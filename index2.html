About us

Services

BirdVision is characterized by the various services it offers to programmers in training. A combination of diverse teams, participation of programmers, contract forms and associated cash flows creates the unique character of the platform.

Interdisciplinary teams

Programmers are given the opportunity to work together within a dynamic and diverse team. Our teams consist of students coming from various disciplines.

Participation

Our programmers have a say in the design and development of the product, both within mutual collaborations and individual work processes.

Cashflow

Programmers will receive a part of the cashflow that results from the developed product that they have worked on. This will be determined in advance of the working processes, and is based on the share that the programmer will provide.

Contract form

Contract opportunities are based on a collaborative internship. In this way, student programmers are also secured of a regular internship agreement in addition to the cash flow.

About us

"The most suitable platform for motivated student programmers where innovative visions come together."

BirdVision is an innovative platform that focuses on the stimulation and practise of ICT-related ideas of students. This platform, created by students themselves, is an initiative to give them a kick-start in their career as a programmer. Within BirdVision, a dynamic and diverse team is put together on a project basis, where freedom of thinking and creativity are key to the achievement of the best end products.

BirdVision gives student programmers the opportunity to gain work experience through an internship contract, in which they can put their own ideas and skills into practice. Student programmers are assured of regular internship allowances, but they will also profit from their own developed product. BirdVision makes it possible for student programmers to gain work experience while doing entrepreneurial activities at the same time!
